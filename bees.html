Apis mellifera

Our bees belong to the family of Apis meelifera, the most common bees in Europe. We use the standard boxes to keep them.

The beehive

55.000 to 60.000 bees live in one beehive. We give them the frames with wax plates in to build the honeycombs.

The queen bee

Can you spot the biggest bee in this picture? That's the queen bee. The queen bee lays up to 200,000 eggs per year.

Can't spot the queen?Don't stress yourself. After all this might be the wrong picture. The queen bee usually gets marked with a spot on her back.

A lot of flying

For one kilo of honey, a bee has to fly a total of 150,000 kilometers in her short life. Each bucket contains 40 kg of honey. That's a lot of flying!
