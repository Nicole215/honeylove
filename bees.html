Apis mellifera

Our bees belong to the family of Apis meelifera, the most common bees in Europe

The beehive

55.000 to 60.000 bees live in one beehive. They are all sisters and a few brothers.

The queen bee

The queen bee lays up to 200,000 eggs per year. She lives up to 5 years.

A lot of flying

For one kilo of honey, a bee has to fly a total of 150,000 kilometers in her short life.
